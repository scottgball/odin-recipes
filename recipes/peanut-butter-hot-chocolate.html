Peanut Butter Hot Choclate

Description

A rich, thick, and full-of-flavor hot chocolate made with only 3 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup unsweetend almond milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegan chocolate chips
  • 2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter
  • (optional) fat-free whipped cream

Steps

  1. Combine almond milk, chocolate chips, and powdered peanut butter in a saucepan.
  2. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring constantly until chocolate chips are melted and peanut butter powder is incorporated.
  3. Pour into a mug and top with fat-free whipped cream if desired.
