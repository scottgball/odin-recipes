Coconut Milk Hot Chocolate

Description

This delicious vegan hot chocolate is made with coconut milk and dark chocolate and a pinch of curry. Plus sugar of course, but you can use other sweeteners as well.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup light coconut milk
  • 1 (1 ounce) square dark chocolate, chopped
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • (optional) 1/8 teaspoon curry powder

Steps

  1. Heat coconut milk in a saucepan over medium-low heat until warm, 3 to 5 minutes.
  2. Add chocolate and sugar; whisk until incorporated and chocolate is melted.
  3. Pour mixture into a blender and add curry powder.
  4. Cover and hold lid down with a potholder; blend until smooth, about 15 seconds.
