Vegan Hot Chocolate

Description

Easy vegan hot chocolate to keep you warm during the winter!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup water
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 cups oatmilk

Steps

  1. Bring water, sugar, and cocoa to a boil in a small saucepan.
  2. Pour in oatmilk and stir until combined and heated through, about 3 minutes.
